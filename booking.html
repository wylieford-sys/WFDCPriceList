Helpful prep

These quick checks help the appointment go smoothly and reduce back-and-forth after the order is submitted.

Inside the home

  • Turn on all lights
  • Open blinds and curtains
  • Clear counters and visible clutter
  • Put away personal items

Outside the home

  • Move cars out of the driveway
  • Close garage doors
  • Tidy patios and entryways
  • Water grass if needed
